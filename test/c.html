Phạm Hoàng Anh – Chief Marketing Strategist July 11, 2026

1. Executive Summary

By 2026, Vietnam’s e-commerce landscape has transitioned from a fragmented growth phase to a mature "Duopoly Era" dominated by Shopee and TikTok Shop (~97% GMV share). The market is no longer driven by raw traffic but by "Precision Growth"—a synthesis of AI-driven personalization, logistics excellence, and cultural storytelling. This strategy outlines the launch and scaling of "AuraHome" (Smart & Green Living) using a Multi-Agent AI system to capture a projected $34B market opportunity. Our focus is shifting from subsidizing prices to optimizing the "Content-to-Cart" journey through automated operations.

2. Market Analysis (Vietnam 2026)

Growth Projections

GMV will reach $31B-$34B with a 22% CAGR. E-commerce now accounts for 15% of total retail.

The Duopoly Split

Shopee (53% share) acts as the "Utility & Fintech" backbone, while TikTok Shop (44% share) dominates "Discovery & Shoppertainment."
