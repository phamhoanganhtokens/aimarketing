1. Executive Summary

By 2026, the Vietnamese real estate market has completed its structural transformation. The era of speculative "land flipping" has ended, replaced by a "Utility-First" paradigm driven by the 2024 Land Law. With GDP growth robust at 7.3%–8.5%, yet constrained by high mortgage rates (11%–14%), our strategy moves away from mass-volume brokerage toward Insight-Driven Asset Management. We position ourselves as the bridge between regulatory transparency and end-user lifestyle aspirations.

2. Market Analysis

Macro Environment

High GDP growth fuels middle-class expansion, yet the 11%–14% interest rate environment creates a "high-barrier" market for speculators.

Regulatory Maturity

Full implementation of digital land registries has institutionalized transparency. Marketing now leads with "Legal-First" messaging.
